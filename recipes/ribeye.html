Cast Iron Ribeye

About

This is a basic walkthrough of how I cook my ribeye steak at home. It is super simple and you only need a few ingredents.

Ingredents

  • Big Ribeye Steak
  • Butter
  • Avocado Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. About 30 minutes before you start take steak out of fridge and salt generously. Season with pepper as well
  2. Get your cast iron pan hot (you might want to open a window, it may get smokey.
  3. add a small amount of avocado oil to the pan
  4. Sear first side of steak for about 4-5 minutes, flip and do the same for the other side
  5. Add butter to pan and baste steak. Continue to flip every 3 minutes until steak reaches desired doneness
  6. For medium-rare use instant read thermometer and pull at 135 degrees fahrenheit
  7. Let steak rest for 5-10 minutes
  8. Enjoy
